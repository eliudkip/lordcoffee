Our Journey

Nova Gardens Limited is a private company founded in 2019 with the goal of selling a varied range of green and roasted coffee farmed by major and small scale farmers in Kenya's coffee and nut growing regions to the global market. It's all about quality and consistency for us at Nova Coffee. This is the feature that distinguishes Nova Coffee as a reliable brand

Our Vision



At Lord Coffee, our journey begins in the heart of Nairobi, Kenya, where we transform the finest Kenyan coffee beans into a symphony of flavors for the world to savor. Experience the rich tapestry of Kenyan coffee culture through our expertly roasted beans and a range of coffee-related accessories.
