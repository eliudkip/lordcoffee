Our Founder

The founder of Loard Coffee, a pioneering female entrepreneur, propelled her passion for coffee into a company specializing in green and roasted beans, as well as a range of coffee accessories. Her visionary approach led to the creation of a diverse selection of premium-quality beans and an expansion into offering meticulously crafted coffee accessories.

With a keen eye for industry dynamics, she championed Loard Coffee's growth, emphasizing innovation and sustainability. Her dedication to excellence shines through in the brand's commitment to delivering top-tier beans and accessories, seamlessly combining artistry and scientific precision in coffee cultivation, roasting, and brewing methods.
